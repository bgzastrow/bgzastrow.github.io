I am a graduate student in the Aerospace Engineering department at the University of Texas at Austin, with a focus on controls. My research focuses on reduced order modeling for coupled aerostructural simulations, and I am advised by Professor Karen Willcox at the Oden Institute for Computational Engineering and Sciences.

I received my B.S. in Engineering Mechanics from the University of Wisconsin - Madison in 2018. While I was an undergraduate, I worked as a co-op student employee for ATA-Engineering in Spring 2015, Fall 2015, and Summer 2016, primarily supporting FEA modeling projects. In Summer 2017, I interned at Ford Motor Company in the brake control algorithms group. After graduating, I worked for ATA-Engineering for three years, where I mostly worked on structural dynamics and solid mechanics modeling.
